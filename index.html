Meet Rosa

Curandera,
Ceremonialist &
Guide

Rosa Diaz de Leon is a sacred feminine guide, curandera, and ceremonialist devoted to the path of the Rose. Her work lives at the intersection of ancestral healing, somatic embodiment, and the ancient wisdom of the Divine Feminine.

Through ceremony, ritual, and deep soul work, Rosa supports women in reclaiming their erotic innocence, healing their womb lineage, and embodying the full spectrum of their feminine nature — from the tender Seed to the radiant Rose Goddess.

Her offerings draw from curanderismo, plant medicine traditions, somatic practices, and the sacred teachings of the Rose lineage.
