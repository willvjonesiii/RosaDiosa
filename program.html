The Path

A journey back to
your sovereign self.

The Rose Goddess Path is a guided, earth-centered journey through the five elements — Earth, Water, Fire, Air, and Spirit — designed to awaken the embodied feminine within you.

Over ten weeks, you move through each elemental stage, meeting a different archetype of the Rose Goddess within yourself, emerging with a deeper connection to your body, your voice, and your sacred power.

Before the Path

The Initiation:
The Rose Dieta

Before the ten-week journey begins, there is an initiation — a sacred two-week period of listening called the Rose Dieta.

Rooted in indigenous traditions, a dieta invites us to slow down and build a relationship with a plant spirit. The rose becomes a teacher of softness, boundaries, beauty, and truth.

The Rose Dieta is not preparation for the work — it is the first act of it.
