The Journey

A sacred path back
to yourself.

The Rose Goddess Path is a 10-week earth-centered journey designed for women who are ready to awaken their bodies, reclaim their pleasure, and embody the full power of the sacred feminine.

You will move through five elemental stages — Earth, Water, Fire, Air, and Spirit — each one a sacred doorway into a deeper layer of yourself. The journey begins with a 2-week Rose Dieta initiation.

"Everything you seek already lives within you."

The Initiation

The Rose
Dieta

Before the journey begins, there is a 2-week period of sacred listening. Rooted in indigenous traditions, a dieta invites you to slow down and build a relationship with a plant spirit.

The rose becomes your teacher of softness, boundaries, beauty, and truth. She asks nothing of you except your presence.

Duration: 2 weeks · Online · Guided by Rosa

✦

The rose teaches

In the stillness of the dieta, the rose reveals herself as teacher. She holds the paradox of the feminine — profound softness and fierce protection, delicate beauty and sharp truth.

🌿 Archetype: The Wild Rose

Air —
Awakening the Voice

The voice is one of the most powerful expressions of the feminine. In this stage, you reclaim your right to be heard — through sound, breath, and the full expression of who you truly are.
